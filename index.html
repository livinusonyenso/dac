Why associations choose Dubai

Dubai Association Centre (DAC) provides a formal framework for professional associations to establish a presence in Dubai. Facilitating licensing and operational support, DAC is jointly established by Dubai Chambers, Dubai Department of Economy and Tourism, and Dubai World Trade Centre.
